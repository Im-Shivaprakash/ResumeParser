Computer Vision Engineer – Fresher (Job Description)

Role Summary:
We are looking for a Computer Vision Engineer (Fresher) who will work on image processing, deep learning–based model development, and deployment of vision systems for real-world applications.

Responsibilities

Work with image and video datasets for preprocessing, annotation, and augmentation

Implement classical computer vision techniques (thresholding, edge detection, feature extraction)

Build and train deep learning models for detection, classification, and segmentation

Evaluate models using metrics like mAP, IOU, accuracy, and F1-score

Optimize models for inference using ONNX, TensorRT, or OpenVINO

Collaborate with the team to integrate vision models into applications or pipelines

Write clean, modular, and well-documented code

Required Skills

Strong understanding of Python

Knowledge of OpenCV, NumPy, Pandas

Basics of Deep Learning frameworks: PyTorch or TensorFlow

Understanding of CNNs, data preprocessing, augmentations

Basic knowledge of object detection algorithms (YOLO, SSD, Faster R-CNN)

Good understanding of image fundamentals (color spaces, filters, morphology)

Ability to work with datasets and write custom preprocessing pipelines

Good to Have

Experience with MediaPipe, OpenVINO, TensorRT, or ONNX

Knowledge of tracking algorithms (SORT, DeepSORT)

Experience with Kaggle projects or personal CV projects

Familiarity with GPU acceleration (CUDA basics)

Education

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, AI/ML, ECE, or related fields
